Preview terms for Command EVE.

Scope

These draft terms describe the preview use of Command EVE, a founder-facing operating layer for AI-native company workflows, worker contracts, review gates and implementation support.

No autonomous production authority

Preview usage does not grant Command EVE permission to publish, spend money, create paid accounts, make legal commitments, process sensitive customer data or perform irreversible actions without explicit human approval.

Implementation-led access

Access during the 0.6.8 preview may be invite-based, manually scoped and delivered through guided setup. Self-serve pricing and general availability may change with later versions.

AI output and review

AI-generated outputs, plans, worker reports, copy, code or operating recommendations require appropriate human review before publication or operational reliance. Command EVE is an orchestration and operating layer, not a replacement for professional judgment.

Billing and cancellation

Preview pricing may be scoped manually. If subscriptions, API credits, BYOK usage or consumable digital goods are introduced, the applicable order form, invoice, app-store rules or platform terms will govern billing and refunds.

Third-party services

Command EVE may coordinate work across third-party tools such as GitHub, Plane, Linear, Vercel, Supabase, OpenAI, Anthropic or other services. Customers remain responsible for their third-party accounts, usage, data and provider charges unless a written agreement says otherwise.
