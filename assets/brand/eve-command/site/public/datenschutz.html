Website and preview intake

Command EVE preview pages are intended to collect only the details needed to prepare a first department-map brief: name, company or website, 14-day goal, email address and explicit consent for that brief.

No public posting, account setup, paid implementation or automated follow-up is triggered by the preview form without human review.

Hosting and logs

The website may be hosted on Vercel or a comparable hosting provider. Technical logs such as IP address, timestamp, requested page and user agent may be processed to deliver, secure and stabilize the service.

Local storage

The site may store local preferences such as interface state, language, theme or demo form progress in your browser. These values are not used for advertising tracking.

Contact requests

If you contact us by email, phone or form, we process the information you provide to respond, evaluate fit and prepare the requested operating-map material.

Your rights

You may request access, correction or deletion of your personal data by contacting privacy@fyn-labs.com.
