# /business-pressure-test

Use this workflow during Company.OS onboarding before recommending automation
or department rollout.

## Public Intake Bridge

AI Business Blueprint may be used before this workflow as a public founder
diagnostic and lead magnet:

```text
https://github.com/MathiasHeinke/ai-business-blueprint
```

If Blueprint worksheet answers exist, use them as input hypotheses only. Still
verify website evidence, current customer behavior, market reality and
Company.OS fit before recommending automation.

## Trigger

- `/business-pressure-test [company or offer]`
- "pressure test this business"
- "is this company ready for Company.OS?"
- "test this offer against the market"
- "what are we missing?"

## Inputs

Ask only for missing critical information:

```text
Company / offer:
Target customer:
What the buyer should do or pay for:
Current traction or current behavior:
Website:
Known competitors:
```

If a website or discovery brief exists, read that first.

## Method

Apply a direct early-stage startup lens:

1. Is the pain intense?
2. Is the buyer clear?
3. Is there urgency?
4. What does the buyer do today?
5. What is the real switching cost?
6. Is the offer differentiated?
7. Can this be validated manually?
8. Does the company have a credible first wedge?
9. Would Company.OS improve a real workflow or just decorate chaos?
10. What would prove the idea false?

## Rules

- Be direct.
- Treat "no competition" as false.
- Current behavior is competition.
- Do not recommend automation before validating the workflow.
- Do not invent market facts.
- If current market data matters, mark it as `needs fresh research`.
- Never make legal, GDPR or AI-Act conclusions. Create a review question.
- Do not promise savings. Return ranges and confidence.
